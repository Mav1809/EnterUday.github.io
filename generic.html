Analysis of occurrence of breast cancer using hierarchical clustering

3rd International Conference on Recent Innovative Trends in Computer Science and Applications [ICRITCSA - 2022]

This paper deals with the prediction of occurrence of cancer disease (Breast) among different age group people and we have used three hierarchical clustering algorithms for our prediction and after prediction, we use the results for comparative analysis among chosen algorithms. The algorithms we have selected are J48, REPTree and Random Tree; all these algorithms have their way of making predictions and benefits of using them. After implementation of the dataset with the chosen algorithm, we found the j48 algorithm with the highest accuracy as compared to the other two algorithms i.e., Random Forest and REPTree. In addition to that j48 avoid the concept of over fitting by the selection of the most suitable parameter for splitting and modelling the hierarchy of prediction which is shown in the article.
